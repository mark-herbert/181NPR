To register Fingerprint of the resident.


1. Click the .

2. Input the Admin Credentials.

3. Select the resident to be registered.

4. Right Click the Finger 1.

5. Click Register.

6. Click .

7. Place the finger to be registered.

8. Click the Validate 1, then place the finger that was register.

       Note: Fingerprint Match will be shown if the finger print are matched, Fingerprint Unmatched will be show if fingerprint are unmatched.

9. Click  the Validate 2, then place the finger that was registered.

       Note: Fingerprint Match will be shown if the finger print are matched, Fingerprint Unmatched will be show if fingerprint are unmatched.

10. Repeat step 6 to 9 for the Fingerprint 2.

11. Click .
